A/B Testing & Conversion Rate Optimization Analysis

A data-driven analysis to identify the best performing website elements and increase user conversion.

INTRODUCTION

In the highly competitive telecommunications sector, customer churn, or the loss of subscribers, poses a significant threat to revenue and growth. Identifying customers who are likely to leave is critical for companies to implement effective retention strategies. Traditional methods often fail to capture the complex relationships within vast customer data. This is where an Artificial Neural Network (ANN) can be a game-changer. By leveraging the power of deep learning, an ANN can analyze intricate patterns in customer behavior, usage data, and billing information to accurately predict which customers are at risk of churning, giving companies a proactive tool to improve customer satisfaction and loyalty.
